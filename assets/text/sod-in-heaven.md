# Sod In Heaven - MPTL Microplastics

![Cover](assets/images/grid/mptl.jpg)

## About

[Bandcamp](https://mptlmicroplastics.bandcamp.com/album/sod-in-heaven),
[Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/album/04Ixr0DaUUWMkoib1b4LfQ)

I co-produced this album with the band between 2024-2025 after being introduced to them by our mutual friend
Ned Powley.

"Plastic Princess" was recorded in my former studio in Cable Street. I have no idea where Dan recorded "Sex/Pol". The remainder of the album was recorded live in session at Hackney Road Studios.

Dan Powley mixed "Sex/Pol" and "Arabic Umlaut", while I mixed the rest and mastered the album.
